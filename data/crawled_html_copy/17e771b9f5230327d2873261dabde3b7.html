Alchemy Intelligent Data Migration Platform Now on SAP® Store

By integrating with SAP S/4HANA, Alchemy from Cognitus delivers AI-powered data migration and conversion capabilities to customers.

Dallas, TX —  12 June 2024 — Cognitus today announced that its intelligent data migration solution is now available on , the online marketplace for SAP and partner offerings. Alchemy integrates with SAP S/4HANA and delivers AI-powered data migration, conversion, and validation capabilities at an accelerated pace to customers.

“Alchemy provides a comprehensive platform to accelerate data migration to SAP S/4HANA for customers across diverse industries”, said SAP Data Migration Practice Lead, Cognitus. “With a user-friendly interface, interactive dashboards, and robust reporting capabilities, the platform empowers users to exercise greater control and accuracy over their data and ensure a smooth migration journey.”

By leveraging the , organizations can achieve a smoother, faster, and more secure transition to SAP S/4HANA Cloud. Some of the key features are:

  • Seamless Data Migration: Alchemy automates and streamlines the data migration process, boosting efficiency by up to 72%, and reducing project timelines and costs.
  • Enhanced Security: Its robust approval workflows and pre-load/post-load validation features ensure secure, accurate data migration to SAP S/4HANA.
  • Minimized business downtimes: With its intelligent data transformation capabilities, Alchemy can empower businesses to maintain near-zero disruption to their operations during the migration process.
  • Improved traceability: Alchemy’s validation hub allows businesses to easily sign off on extractions, filters, cleansing, mapping, transformation, pre-loads, and post-loads, ensuring complete control and transparency.
  • Real-Time Insights: The migration console offers real-time insights into the process, optimizing the efficacy and efficiency of data loads, batching, organization, and overall execution.

SAP Store, found at store.sap.com, delivers a simplified and connected digital customer experience for finding, trying, buying, and renewing more than 2,300 solutions from SAP and its partners. There, customers can find the SAP solutions and SAP-validated solutions they need to grow their business. And for each purchase made through the SAP Store, SAP will plant a tree.
