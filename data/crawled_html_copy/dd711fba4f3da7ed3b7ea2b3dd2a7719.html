What to Expect

Learn key strategies to beat the unprecedented competition for talent in the aerospace industry, enhance workforce development, and the real business impact associated with a culture of allyship, inclusion, and engagement. Join us as we discuss:

The importance of creating informed strategies to attract and retain the best talent in a competitive market.

Trends and insights shaping the future workforce in the aerospace industry.

Proven methods for cultivating and enhancing employee skills and potential.

How mentoring programs can add value and positively impact career growth.

Insights into coaching techniques to improve performance and leadership.

The role of advocacy in promoting a supportive and inclusive work environment.
