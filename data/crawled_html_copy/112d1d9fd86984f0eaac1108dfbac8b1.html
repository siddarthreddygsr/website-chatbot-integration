Adopt Cloud ERP Quickly and Confidently – What GROW with SAP means

In today’s ever-changing and competitive market, midsize businesses need to be able to transform quickly and efficiently.

What is GROW with SAP?

, but what is it? It is a pre-packaged and comprehensive cloud ERP offering that’s designed for midmarket businesses to provide agility, scalability, and innovation. In today’s ever-changing and competitive market, midsize businesses need to be able to transform quickly and efficiently. The announcement of GROW with SAP is really disrupting the status-quo by making it possible to digitally transform to the cloud in as little as four to six weeks.

GROW with SAP comes after a highly successful RISE with SAP offering and is specifically designed to help midsize companies harness the proven benefits of cloud ERP now and into the future.  

Why does GROW with SAP matter?

Today’s market requires quick implementation, quick time-to-value, and most importantly, digital transformation that actually works. GROW with SAP is a complete offering of solutions, adoption acceleration services, community, and learning so any size company can successfully adopt cloud ERP:

  • Ready-to-run cloud ERP solution: grow without limits and build your own breakthroughs with a ready-to-run cloud ERP. Leverage SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition, accelerate your business with the latest industry best practices and continuous innovation.
  • Adoption and acceleration services: expedite your cloud ERP rollout with proven tools and methodology. With a predictable scope and timeframe, you can minimize surprises through a proven deployment service.
  • Community and learning: engage and learn in a collaborative, open community of SAP experts, partners, and customers. You can build skills and improve adoption across your team with flexible learning and training tailored to your goals.

What does it mean for your company?

Arguably the biggest advantage to GROW with SAP is it’s swift implementation, with a technical go-live in as little as four to six weeks. SAP has combined three of its strongest assets into a powerhouse program to help customers move to the cloud:

  • SAPS/4HANA Cloud: enables customers to quickly and efficiently scale their business
  • SAP Business Technology Platform: helps customers innovate their business
  • Preconfigured industry best practices: speed up customer implementations to gain a competitive advantage

Benefits

Run with industry best practices.

SAP has embedded pre-configured best-practices into GROW with SAP from their 50+ years of experience and defining best-in-class, industry-specific processes. Businesses can now adopt proven processes and get ahead of their competition with the help of embedded AI and automation capabilities.

Build your own breakthroughs.

GROW with SAP helps you meet your budget requirements without financial surprises or delays. You can start with the right solution to fit your company’s size and business needs at a predictable cost and within an expected time frame.

Grow without limits. 

Because the software-as-a-service (SaaS) solution is continuously updated, your system remains current. Built-in security and regular compliance updates offer peace of mind.

Go live with confidence.

GROW with SAP, offers nearly 80% of pre-configured solutions that are focused on best-practices and industry methodologies to make going live quick and easy, while leaving about 20% available for customization to how you do business. In addition, it’s designed to scale and support extensive supply chains, complex service offerings, and bold sustainability goals.

Customization without code.

With the bundling of SAP Business Technology Platform (BTP), GROW with SAP makes the best of pre-configuration and customization. SAP BTP empowers businesses to create their unique processes and solutions tailored to their individual needs through customer apps or processes in a cloud-native way.

What’s included?

The GROW with SAP offering includes a robust line-up, including products, best-practice support, adoption acceleration services, a community, and learning opportunities to help you use SAP S/4HANA® Cloud, public edition with speed, predictability, and continuous innovation. GROW with SAP combines the benefits of advanced cloud technology with extensive industry experience:

  • Inclusive offering with solutions, services, community, and learning access
  • SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition – a ready-to-run cloud ERP solution
  • SAP® Business Technology Platform
  • Packaged activation services for SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition
  • Expert and user support through SAP Community and the SAP Learning portfolio

What’s the difference between GROW with SAP and RISE with SAP?

In short, GROW with SAP is tailored towards midsize companies looking for a quick, out-of-the-box solution with expert support and community resources. RISE with SAP offers a more widespread and flexible suite of services for businesses of all sizes, truly allowing customers to customize their path towards digital transformation.   

With this new GROW with SAP announcement, SAP S/4HANA has never been more accessible.
