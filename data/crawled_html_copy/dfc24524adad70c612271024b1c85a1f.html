What is revenue management software and how can it benefit your business?

Revenue management software is a specialized tool used by businesses to optimize pricing, go-to-market programs, and leveraging inventory and distribution strategies to maximize revenue and profitability.

This includes setting optimal prices based on factors such as demand, seasonality, and customer segments, as well as enabling dynamic pricing adjustments in real time to maximize revenue and profitability.  It also includes promotion management, rebates, chargebacks management (if working with distributors), as well as other peripheral processes that are all part of the pricing waterfall.

Chargebacks: Take Control

What are Chargebacks?

Chargebacks, not to be confused with credit card chargebacks or deductions, are a very common business transaction between the Distributor and the Manufacturer. A chargeback is an amount claimed by a distributor from a manufacturer or vendor for the difference between the distributor’s initial acquisition price and the mutually agreed-upon price for products or services sold to a specific end customer or partner.

Most manufacturers track distributor chargebacks in Excel and communicate via e-mail.  If you are a distributor, you must submit your chargebacks to the manufacturer to be made whole, timely, and accurate to expedite the reimbursement process.

Vistex® Revenue management software automates the chargeback process, ensuring accuracy and efficiency. It matches chargebacks against contracts and agreements, reducing manual errors and administrative costs. The result? No pricing discrepancies help to streamline chargebacks and prevent cost leakage.

Here are some benefits of automating chargebacks:

  • Reduced Errors: Automation minimizes manual data entry errors.
  • Faster Processing: Streamlined workflows accelerate the chargeback process.
  • Improved Relationships: Accurate handling of chargebacks strengthens partner relationships.
  • Enhanced Profits: Accelerate incoming cash flow, enable chargeback accounting, and reduce your chargebacks DSO.
Billbacks: Avoid Paying Double

What are Billbacks?

A billback is the process of validating compensation by a manufacturer from one of its channel partners. The reason may involve the amount paid by a manufacturer or vendor to a distributor for the difference between the distributor’s initial acquisition price and the mutually agreed-upon price for products/services sold to a specific end customer.

As a manufacturer, or Intellectual Property Owner (i.e. such as in the case of High-Tech industries), then you must ensure the Distributor submission of a Chargeback to you is both, accurate (in terms of dollars) and not duplicate (in terms of inventory and prior submissions).

Vistex® software simplifies the management of billbacks by automating calculations and ensuring compliance with promotional agreements. It provides real-time visibility into billback activities and accruals.

Here are some benefits of simplifying billbacks’ management with Vistex®:

  • Accurate Calculations: Automation ensures precise billback amounts.
  • Improved Tracking: Track sales-in and sales-out transactions, selling price, and inventory levels to enable advance billback claim adjudication.
  • Enhanced Visibility: Real-time tracking improves financial forecasting.
  • Cost Savings: Efficient processing reduces administrative expenses.
Rebates: Maximize Incentive Programs

What are Rebates?

Rebates are retrospective payments given to customers as an incentive for purchasing in volume or meeting specific sales targets. Managing rebates can be complex due to varying eligibility criteria and conditions.

Vistex® rebate management module automates the entire rebate lifecycle, from defining rebate programs to tracking performance and processing payments. It integrates seamlessly with your existing systems, ensuring data consistency.

Automating rebate management can help create a swift impression of your brand for your target audience. Here are some benefits:

  • Increased Accuracy: Automation ensures rebate calculations are error-free.
  • Improved Cash Flow: Timely processing of rebates enhances cash flow management, in the case of purchasing rebates.
  • Customer Loyalty: Well-managed rebate programs incentivize repeat business, such as in the case of sales rebates.
