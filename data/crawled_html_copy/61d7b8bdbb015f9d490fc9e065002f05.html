Cognitus’ Aftermarket Solution Now Available on SAP® Store

By integrating with SAP S/4HANA, Cognitus’ Industry Solution for Aftermarket Services (CIS-AM) from Cognitus delivers total asset visibility to customers.

Dallas, TX —  4 July 2024 — Cognitus today announced that its is now available on the , the online marketplace for SAP and partner offerings. CIS-AM integrates with SAP S/4HANA and delivers total asset visibility to enhance asset lifecycle and streamline aftermarket processes for customers.

“In the fast-paced aftermarket industry, total asset visibility is the key to extending asset lifecycle and maximizing value,” said , Director of Aftermarket Services, Cognitus. “CIS-AM is a robust enhancement to SAP’s S/4HANA maintenance capabilities that empowers key stakeholders with the ability to obtain comprehensive visibility into each stage of the asset lifecycle to ensure that the item can meet its mission when called upon and mitigate potential risks.”

The revolutionary solution by Cognitus builds a holistic digital ecosystem for the to offer multiple business benefits:

  • Optimized Turnaround: The solution expedites maintenance execution processes, ensuring swift asset turnarounds without compromising quality, and fostering efficiency throughout operations.
  • Solution Flexibility: CIS-AM has enabled key steps in the Aftermarket/MRO process to be executed in multiple ways – including and excluding items based on asset complexity and enabling configurable pop-ups to speed the processing of some transaction steps
  • Enhanced Asset Optimization: CIS-AM maximizes the utilization of historical maintenance data, facilitating trend analysis and empowering decision-making tools for effective and informed decision support.
  • Seamless Integration: CIS-AM seamlessly integrates with your existing data systems and SAP S/4HANA to provide a holistic view that optimizes total asset management opportunities, enhancing overall operational efficiency.
  • Modernized User Experience: The solution elevates the SAP landscape by replacing older technologies with a contemporary user experience. It introduces role-based screens integrated with SAP S/4HANA, Fiori, and other cloud-based solutions tailored to the specific needs of key Aftermarket users.
