Cognitus Achieves GROW With SAP Designation

Cognitus today announced it has achieved the GROW with SAP designation. The GROW with SAP offering helps midsize companies adopt cloud ERP. This offering provides products, best-practice support, adoption acceleration services, a community, and learning opportunities to help customers move to SAP S/4HANA Cloud, public edition with speed, predictability, and continuous innovation.

Cognitus has fulfilled SAP’s strict eligibility criteria to achieve the GROW with SAP designation, which includes go-to-market readiness and competency status.

“GROW with SAP will make it incredibly easy for mid-market businesses to leverage the power of Cloud ERP to grow, scale, and collaborate with a global ecosystem at minimal costs,” said Pat Sathi,CEO, Cognitus. “Together with our qualified partner package solutions for GROW with SAP, customers can access Cloud ERP in scope, in budget and on time for rapid business transformation!”

Cognitus has introduced the first ever global partner package solution, building a clear implementation path for customers to adopt cloud ERP with GROW with SAP. The key differentiators of this package include:

  • Increased predictability of costs and operations
  • Fast and simplified cloud ERP adoption
  • Accessible and scalable options for small and midsize businesses
  • Strategic support with industry best practices
  • GROW with SAP is underpinned by its predictable, cost effective and rapid implementation and the ability to continue to scale into the future.
