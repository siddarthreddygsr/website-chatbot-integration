Deployment Strategies for SAP S/4HANA

Greenfield Approach

The Greenfield method offers a fresh start, allowing businesses to build from the ground up with SAP S/4HANA. This approach is ideal for companies seeking to revamp their IT infrastructure and business processes from diverse legacy systems and provides a clean slate for innovation and alignment with modern best practices.

Brownfield Approach

Tailored for organizations looking to upgrade existing SAP systems, the Brownfield approach enables a smooth transition to SAP S/4HANA. It preserves valuable customizations and data, offering a cost-effective modernization method without reinventing the wheel.

Bluefield Approach

The Bluefield strategy facilitates selective data transition and phased implementation. This approach is perfect for businesses requiring flexibility, allowing for simultaneous operation of legacy systems and gradual integration of SAP S/4HANA features.
