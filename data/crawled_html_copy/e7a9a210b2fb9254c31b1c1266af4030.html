SAP Condition Contract Management (CCM)

Automate settlement management for unparalleled efficiency and cohesion

Integrated into the SAP S/4HANA, SAP Settlement Management (or SAP Condition Contract Management) offers an advanced architecture and adaptability to diverse financial scenarios. It streamlines rebate management, manages complex rebate agreements, and automates calculations, significantly reducing data footprint and enhancing system performance for diverse industries.

Empower Your Rebate, Commission, and Royalty Processes with SAP and Cognitus

Create settlement documents with configured pricing, condition contract maintenance, and contract settlement functions, and process settlements to align with business needs.

Efficiently calculate rebates and accruals using business volume determination and store agreement details, such as prices, validity, and conditions, ensuring accurate rebate calculation.

Leverage standard reports for reviewing condition contract conditions and settlement-related data, and exercise enhanced control and transparency over rebate management.

Customize the system by defining pricing conditions, setting parameters for contract types, and configuring settlement processing settings.

Automate the selection of business volumes for condition contracts and allow contracts to be settled manually or automatically, improving efficiency.

Handle diverse business scenarios, including sales and purchase rebates, royalties, commissions, and customs, and quickly adapt to dynamic market conditions.

Key Benefits of SAP Condition Contract Management

Maximized Data Efficiency

SAP CCM eliminates outdated rebate index tables, optimizes data storage, and enhances financial data management to reduce redundancy and improve access to critical information.

Cost-Effectiveness

Included in the SAP S/4HANA license, SAP CCM eliminates the need for external solutions, reducing overall costs, simplifying financial management, and improving ROI.

Seamless Integration

Built within SAP S/4HANA, SAP CCM facilitates real-time data processing and reporting, ensuring up-to-date financial data and enhancing decision-making across the organization.

Real-time Insights

SAP CCM provides immediate access to real-time insights into actual business volumes and accruals that enhance decision-making and operational efficiency.

Improved Agility

Tailor financial agreements to fit specific needs such as customer rebates, vendor commissions, and royalty payments, and leverage customized solutions for various business models and diverse requirements.

Enhanced User Experience

SAP CCM incorporates SAP Fiori apps to enhance usability and accessibility, simplifying complex processes and improving user productivity and satisfaction.
